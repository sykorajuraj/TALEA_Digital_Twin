The project aims to contribute to the development of a civic digital twin for the city of Bologna, Italy. 
Digital twin merging geographical and environmental information with socio-demographic data. 
The goal is to explore how various policies (housing, mobility, district planning) can affect 
different social groups - and then study ways to mitigate this impact, acting to increase 
equity and fairness.

## Digital Twin for multi-modal mobility Simulation 

Simulate mobility flows under heat stress scenarios, integrating public transport and active mobility.
